Privacy Policy

Last Updated: February 4, 2026

TL;DR: Everything runs in your browser. Your images never leave your device. No tracking, no accounts, no bs.

What We Collect

Short answer: nothing.

  • Your images are processed entirely on your device using the browser's Canvas API — they are never uploaded anywhere
  • The ZIP file is generated locally and downloaded straight to your device
  • We store a download counter in your browser's localStorage only to enforce the daily limit client-side

What We DON'T Collect

  • No user accounts
  • No email addresses
  • No IP addresses
  • No cookies for tracking
  • No analytics (Google Analytics, etc.)
  • No databases
  • No server — this site is fully static
  • Google AdSense — for displaying ads cuz i need those $$$ (still not there yet)

How Your Images Are Handled

  1. You pick an image — it stays on your device the whole time
  2. The browser resizes and composites it onto the GD templates using Canvas
  3. JSZip packages everything into a ZIP file in memory
  4. The ZIP gets downloaded directly to your device
  5. Nothing was ever sent anywhere

Third-Party Services

We load a couple of external scripts to make the site work:

  • Nerd Fonts CDN (icons)
  • JSZip via cdnjs (ZIP generation)
  • canvas-confetti via jsDelivr (the fun stuff)
  • Ko-fi widget (only loads if you click donate)

Social links in the footer go to YouTube, Twitch, GitHub, and Discord — those are just links, nothing loads unless you click them.

Your Rights

Since nothing gets collected or stored on our end, there's nothing to request, delete, or export. You're completely anonymous.
